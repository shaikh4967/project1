We are LONIX

Lonix Ceramic has been a specialist tiles manufacturer and distributor since 2021 and was established by Mr. Bharat Dalsaniya. They specialize in manufacturing fine quality GVT/PGVT tiles for walls and floors with different and unique designs, colors, and sizes. As designers and creators of ceramics, we are passionate about exploring new tiles with the latest materials, colors, and textures. These are reflected in our comprehensive product ranges. With our innovative and creative approach, we guarantee you find a better quality ceramic tile for your style and space, whether you're renovating, building, or simply looking to refresh your decor.
